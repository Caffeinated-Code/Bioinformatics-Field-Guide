What makes this useful

Short guides with reusable mental models.

  • Beginner-friendly explanations without flattening the science.
  • Practical warnings about common analysis mistakes.
  • Saveable checklists, maps, and interpretation rubrics.
  • Links to tools, references, and reproducible resources.
