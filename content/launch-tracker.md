## Publication Readiness Definition

A post is ready only when:
- The science is accurate.
- The language is clear.
- The article can be read in about 7 minutes.
- The post has at least one visual, table, or original reusable asset.
- Citations and credits are present.
- No internal planning or prompt text appears.
- The expert review checklist is complete.
- The EB-1A value is explicit but not awkwardly advertised to readers.
